ABOUT

Hi there! I'm a UX Designer and Researcher specializing in qualitative research and user requirement analysis, having recently completed my Master of Information degree at the University of Toronto. Previously, I earned joint Bachelor degrees in Education and Music, as well as a Quebec teaching license at McGill University. My interests lie in designing for social good, particularly in education and healthcare.

I currently work for Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre designing for SunnyCare, the hospital's main clinical information platform used by clinical staff and medical professionals.
